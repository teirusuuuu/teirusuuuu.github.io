Reaching The Door

As you reach closer to the door, you hear some of the same ambience from before, but now more muffled, as if it's coming from behind the door. A very vibrant blue lighting can be seen from underneath, and above the door. You also feel a cool, calm breeze eminating from whatever is behind the door. You're now interested and considering opening the door, or leaving it be and turning back..

Open The Door. Turn Back.
